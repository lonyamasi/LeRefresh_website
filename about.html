We are the ultimate relaxation destination

At Le Refresh, we believe in the power of self-care and the importance of taking time for yourself. Our spa is designed to be a sanctuary of peace and tranquility, where you can escape the stresses of daily life and indulge in self-care. Our team of skilled therapists is dedicated to providing you with the highest level of service and care, ensuring that your experience with us is nothing short of exceptional. We offer a wide range of services, including massages, facials, body treatments, and more, all designed to help you relax and rejuvenate. We use only the highest quality products and techniques to ensure that you receive the best possible care. Our skilled therapists are trained in a variety of techniques, from traditional Swedish massages to more specialized treatments like hot stone therapy and aromatherapy.
